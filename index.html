The Ather 450X comes with two power variants. The first has a 3.7 kWh battery that gives you a 161 km IDC Range. The second has a 2.9 kWh battery that gives you a 126 km IDC Range.

The Ather 450X electric scooter is available starting at Rs. 799,900. Visit your nearest showroom or call +94 77 755 3355 for more details.

All Ather scooters are available for purchase in Sri Lanka at the flagship store in Maradana or through authorized dealers islandwide.

Riding an Ather 450X is pure joy. With its low centre of gravity and sturdy aluminium frame chassis, it stands out as one of the best electric scooters on the road. Accelerating from 0 to 40 km/h in just 3.3 seconds and reaching a top speed of 90 km/h, it’s built for both quick city rides and smooth journeys on any road in Sri Lanka. Supported by Ather’s expanding fast-charging grid across the country, the 450X offers a range of up to 161 km on a single charge, giving you the freedom to travel farther without worry. Don’t just take our word for it — book a test ride at our flagship store in Maradana or through authorized dealers islandwide and experience the difference yourself.
